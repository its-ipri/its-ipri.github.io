About the conference

Welcome to the XXI International Scientific and Practical Conference "Information Technologies and Security" (ITS-2021). The conference will be held online on 9 December 2021. Discussion will focus on the relevant issues of information security, analytics, and risk management. We invite representatives of academia, higher educational institutions, government bodies, and business to participate in the conference.
